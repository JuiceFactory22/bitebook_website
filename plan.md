Plan/ Prompt 


You are designing a website for a local food coupon service called BiteBook.  

BiteBook sells books of coupons Promoting local restaurants.   each month a different promotion is put on centering around a specific type of food.    the website should be modern feeling and focus on an easy user experience and navigation.  

 highlight our upcoming events as well as previous events.   leave space for more prominent placement of our partner restaurants. 

 the goal of bike book is to sell coupon books.   we want to convert visitors of the website to either sign up as a partner  restaurant or to buy one of our promotional coupon books.  

 coupon books cost $29.99 a piece and feature promotional deals from upwards of 30 local restaurants presenting the buyer with a significant value relative to the cost they pay.  


 bitebook services many major metropolitan areas across the United States.   let's start with building out the Boston metro area.   later we can replicate that structure for other Metro areas around the US.  

 each month is a different promotion coupons are valid for 30 days only.    November is Taco month December is breakfast sandwich month January is pizza month and February is burgers.  

 please highlight that coupon books go fast and the number of books is limited.    a great benefit to our partner restaurants as well as a great way to try new food and find your new favorite place to eat.  


 please make sure to use proper markup schema following best SEO practices for all your content also utilize vest current SEO practices.  include a site map.   name all images using best SEO practices.    reformat and compress images in order to encourage the best page load speed possible without sacrificing quality.   refer to best SEO practices when it comes to any images on page.  

 you can browse other websites and similar services in order to learn more about how to construct this website to fit our ideal customer persona.
